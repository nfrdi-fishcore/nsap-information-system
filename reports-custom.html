How to Use Custom Report Builder
1
Select Date Range and Region

Choose the "From Date" and "To Date" for your report, and optionally filter by Region. These parameters define the data scope.

2
Choose Fields to Include

Select the fields you want to include in your report by checking the boxes. You can select multiple fields such as Date, Region, Species, Catch Volume, etc.

3
Add Filters (Optional)

Click "Add Filter" to create custom filters for your data. Select a field, choose an operator, and enter a value to filter the results.

4
Set Grouping and Sorting

Optionally group your data by a field and sort by another field in ascending or descending order to organize your report.

5
Generate and Export

Click "Generate Report" to create your custom report. You can save your configuration as a template for future use, and export the results to CSV, Excel, or PDF.
