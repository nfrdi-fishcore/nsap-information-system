How to Generate Monthly Report
1
Select Date Range

Choose the "From Date" and "To Date" to specify the time period for your report. These fields are required.

2
Apply Filters (Optional)

Optionally filter the report by Region, Landing Center, or Fishing Ground. Leave these as "All" to include all data in the selected date range.

3
Generate Report

Click the "Generate Report" button to create your monthly report. The report will include summary statistics, charts, and detailed data tables.

4
Export Report (Optional)

After generating the report, you can export it to CSV, Excel, or PDF format using the export buttons at the top of the report.
