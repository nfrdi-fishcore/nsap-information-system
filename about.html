Learn more about the National Stock Assessment Program Information System

Overview

The National Stock Assessment Program (NSAP) Information System is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline and enhance the collection, management, and analysis of fisheries data across the Philippines. Developed under the auspices of the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources (BFAR) and the National Fisheries Research and Development Institute (NFRDI), this system serves as a critical tool for monitoring and assessing fish stocks to support sustainable fisheries management.

The system enables authorized personnel to efficiently record, track, and analyze fishing activities, catch data, vessel information, and species data, providing valuable insights for evidence-based decision-making in fisheries management and conservation efforts.

Key Features

  • Data Entry Management: Comprehensive forms for recording sampling days, gear unloads, vessel unloads, vessel catches, and sample length measurements
  • Reference Tables: Centralized management of landing centers, fishing grounds, fishing effort units, species, gear types, and vessel information
  • Analytics Dashboard: Interactive charts and visualizations for trend analysis, comparative analysis, and performance metrics
  • Reporting System: Generate comprehensive reports including monthly reports, regional reports, species-focused reports, and custom report builder
  • Role-Based Access Control: Secure access management with different permission levels for Superadmin, Admin, Encoder, and Viewer roles
  • Data Export: Export data to multiple formats including CSV, Excel, and PDF for further analysis and sharing
  • User Management: Comprehensive user administration tools for managing system access and permissions
  • Mobile Responsive: Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

System Information

Security

The system implements robust security measures including authentication, role-based access control, and data encryption to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of fisheries data.

Data Management

Built on modern cloud infrastructure with secure database storage, ensuring reliable data persistence and backup capabilities for critical fisheries information.

Analytics

Advanced analytics capabilities provide insights into catch trends, species distribution, gear performance, and regional comparisons to support data-driven fisheries management.

Collaboration

Multi-user support enables teams across different regions to collaborate effectively, with role-based permissions ensuring appropriate access levels for each user.

Purpose & Mission

The NSAP Information System is designed to support the National Stock Assessment Program's mission of providing accurate, timely, and comprehensive fisheries data for sustainable resource management. By digitizing and streamlining data collection processes, the system aims to:

  • Improve the accuracy and efficiency of fisheries data collection
  • Enable real-time monitoring and analysis of fishing activities
  • Support evidence-based decision-making in fisheries management
  • Facilitate data sharing and collaboration among stakeholders
  • Contribute to the sustainable management of Philippine fisheries resources
